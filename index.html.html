The Risk Landscape

Most Organizations Are Using AI
Without Proper Oversight.

Shadow AI adoption, ungoverned data flows, and missing control frameworks expose organizations to risks leadership often doesn't know exist.

Hidden AI Usage Across Teams

Employees adopt AI tools without IT or leadership visibility. You likely don't know the full scope of what's in use.

Uncontrolled Data Exposure

Sensitive data enters AI systems without classification, consent controls, or data governance policies in place.

Missing Governance Policies

Most organizations lack formal AI use policies, acceptable use frameworks, and accountability structures.

Weak Audit Visibility

Without audit trails for AI actions and outputs, organizations can't demonstrate control under regulatory inquiry.

Growing Compliance Gaps

Regulatory scrutiny of AI is accelerating. Organizations without documented governance are already falling behind.

Shadow AI Proliferation

Unapproved AI platforms accumulate rapidly. Each represents a potential breach, compliance violation, or liability.

The Risk Landscape

Most Organizations Are Using AI
Without Proper Oversight.

Shadow AI adoption, ungoverned data flows, and missing control frameworks are exposing organizations to risks that leadership often doesn't know exist.

👁️

Hidden AI Usage Across Teams

Employees are adopting AI tools without visibility or control from IT or leadership. You likely don't know the full scope of what's in use.

⚠️

Uncontrolled Data Exposure

Sensitive data is being fed into AI systems without proper classification, consent controls, or data governance policies in place.

📋

Missing Governance Policies

Most organizations lack formal AI use policies, acceptable use frameworks, and accountability structures for AI-generated decisions.

🔍

Weak Audit Visibility

Without audit trails for AI actions and outputs, organizations can't demonstrate control or respond confidently to regulatory inquiries.

⚖️

Growing Compliance Gaps

Regulatory scrutiny of AI is accelerating. Organizations without documented governance are already falling behind compliance expectations.

🌑

Shadow AI Tool Proliferation

Unapproved AI platforms accumulate rapidly. Each one represents a potential data breach, compliance violation, or liability exposure.
